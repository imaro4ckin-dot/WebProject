Bicycle hiking in outside of city

By Rodrigo | Published: October 12, 2026 | Nature

Getting out of the city doesn't have to cost a fortune. Last weekend, our team decided to skip the expensive train tickets and just use our bikes to explore the outer nature reserves.

The Route

We started near the university campus and headed straight for the river trail. The paths are mostly flat for the first 5 kilometers, making it a very easy ride even if you aren't super athletic. Once you pass the old bridge, the dirt trails begin!

Our Budget Breakdown

Because we are on a strict student budget, we planned ahead to keep costs near zero:

  • Snacks: €5.00 (Supermarket sandwiches, bananas, and a cheap pack of cookies)
  • Bike Maintenance: €0.00 (We pumped our tires for free at the campus station)
  • Viewpoint Entry: Free! Nature doesn't charge admission.

Pro Travel Tip:

Always pack plenty of water and a basic repair kit. Getting a flat tire 10 kilometers out without a patch kit is the only way to ruin a perfectly cheap trip!
